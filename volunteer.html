Volunteer at Our Sixth Annual Banquet

In order to make this banquet a success, we need your help! Please see below for available volunteer positions.

This is our largest fundraising event of the year, and we need plenty of volunteers. Below are the listed volunteer positions we need filled. Please read through and determine which position(s) you would like to and are able to help with.

Auction Setup

4:00 - 5:00
2 people

Will assist the Auction Coordinator to set up the silent auction area.

Reception Setup

3:00 - 5:00
2 people

Will help with setting up the reception area. This includes putting up signage and decorations and preparing the food and wine stations in reception area.

Registration Table

5:00 - 7:15
1 person

Will assist the Registration Leader to check in volunteers and direct them to appropriate stations as well as register attendees. Must dress semi-formally.

Food Preparation

4:30 - 7:30
5 people

Will work with Food Prep Leader to prepare food trays for the reception and make sure that food and wine (and other beverages) are ready and available for Reception Servers during the course of the reception.

Reception Wine Servers

5:00 - 7:00
2 people

Will serve wine to banquet guests during the reception. Must wear a white shirt with a collar and black pants or skirt.

Reception Food & Drink Servers

5:00 - 7:00
3 people

Will make sure that reception food and non-alcoholic beverages are well-stocked during the reception. Must wear a white shirt with a collar and black pants or skirt.

Banquet Servers

5:00 - 9:30
18 people

Will help set up tables in main banquet room and serve food and drink to guests. Must wear a white shirt with a collar and black pants or skirt.

Photographer

5:00 - 9:30
1 person

Will take photos of silent auction, reception, and main banquet. Note that this person will be able to attend the reception and banquet. Must dress semi-formally.

Kitchen Clean-Up

7:30 - 11:00
6 people

Will work with Kitchen Clean-up Leader to clean dishes from the reception and main banquet and make sure that clean dishes are organized packed in cars for return to CAA office or rental location.

Room Clean-Up

9:30 - 11:00
6 people

Will work with Room Clean-up Leader to ensure that banquet tables and rooms are clean and decorations and supplies are organized and packed in cars for return to CAA office. May assist with kitchen clean-up as well.

Please Volunteer!

Food will be provided for volunteers, and there may also be leftovers, so all volunteers staying until 9 pm can bring a container for food if you would like to take some home. Please respond indicating what position(s) you can help out with. Please do not hesitate to ask if you have any questions. If you'd like to volunteer, contact Nina Shimmin at nina.shimmin@exploreveg.org or 763-807-4811.

Please invite your parents, co-workers, or friends to the banquet. We are looking for more donors and attendees. Please direct them to the main banquet page.

Thanks in advance for your help and we look forward to hearing back from you and seeing you at the banquet.
