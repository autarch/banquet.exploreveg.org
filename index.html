Join Us for Our Annual Banquet

Please join us on Thursday, April 8, 2010, as Compassionate Action for Animals celebrates advocating for farm animals at our Sixth Annual CAA Banquet!

Focusing on action, education, and outreach to inspire and promote veganism, CAA touches hundreds of human lives and saves thousands of animal lives. Through the dedicated efforts of CAA volunteers, thousands of leaflets are distributed to increase awareness of the plight of factory farm animals, hundreds of individuals pledge to try vegetarianism, social gatherings are held to bring like-minded people together, and inspirational speakers from across the country educate the public about those who cannot speak for themselves.

The event begins with a reception featuring a selection of delicious hors d'ouevres, and complimentary wine. During the reception, we will also have a silent auction featuring artwork, gift baskets, services from local vendors, and more.

The Program

  • 5:30-7:00 - Reception and silent auction with hors d'ouevres and wine
  • 7:00-8:30 - Dinner
  • 8:30-8:50 - Presentation by CAA
  • 8:50-9:30 - Dessert

The Venue

This year's banquet will once again be held at Coffman Memorial Union on the East Bank of the University of Minnesota's Minneapolis Campus.

The reception will be held in the President's Room, with dinner in the Mississipi Room. Both rooms are on the third floor of the building.

Coffman Memorial Union on Google Maps.

Parking is available behind the building on Delaware St SE.

The Food

This year, our dinner features gourmet vegan catering from Ciree Linsemann of Vegan Fever. The dinner will begin with bread and salad, followed by a choice of soup and entree, all served with your choice of wine or juice. After a brief presentation by CAA, you will enjoy a decadent vegan dessert.

Because you have a choice of soups and entrees, this year we are asking you to select your soup and entree in advance when you purchase your ticket.

The Soups

After bread and salad, you will be served your choice of soup.

Meyer Lemon Crème of Mustard Greens, topped with Toasted Hazelnuts

This tart and floral soup with essence of spring is balanced by rich, nutty accents.

 

Roasted Garlic Peanut with Cinnamon Croutons

Galanga, kefir lime and coconut make this creamy soup tropical, complex and rich.

 

The Entrees

Finally comes the entree. Choose from Cuban or French-inspired delicacies.

Cuban Tamale Pie with Mango Tomatillo de Gallo and Red Pepper Cheddar Truffle

This succulent pie is one layer of corn, delicata squash and potato tamale and the other ancho peppers, cashews and chocolate, complimented by tropical de gallo and a zesty pepper truffle.

 

Smoked Cheddar Quenelle with Horseradish Fraiche and Tart Apple Chutney

This smoky cheddar, potato and cabbage slivers dumpling is baked to perfection and complimented by fresh horseradish crème and piquant chutney.
